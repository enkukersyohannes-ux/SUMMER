classic pancakes

These classic pancakes are soft, fluffy, and easy to make. They are perfect for breakfast and can be served with maple syrup, honey, fresh fruits, or butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1½ cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. In another bowl, beat the eggs and add the milk, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix gently.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour about ¼ cup of batter onto the pan.
  6. Cook until bubbles appear, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.
